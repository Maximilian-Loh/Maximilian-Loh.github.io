Sketching Workshop

Featuring cartoonist and illustrator Tom

Indulge in the theraputical and stress-free drawing while learning a things or of the art from Times Newspaper's cartoonist and illustrator Tom, who has been in the business for 5 years.

At this workskop, you will learn how to:
  • sketch a basic human model
  • understand how light and shadow works on a drawing
  • draw basic features (e.g. eyes, mouth and hands)
